ABOUT

Built for Educators Who Value Time

Manual roll calls waste valuable teaching time. MC Attendance Box replaces the clipboard with deep learning — identifying students by face, marking attendance automatically, and storing everything in the cloud. Built with React 19, Firebase, and the Face API.

FEATURES

Everything You Need

AI Face Recognition

Deep learning-powered identification via Face API. Accurate across lighting conditions.

Real-Time Scanning

Live webcam feed marks attendance as students enter the frame. No intervention needed.

Student Registration

Enroll with name, ID, and biometric face capture in under 30 seconds.

Monthly Dashboard

Visual analytics with month navigation. Track trends and catch absences instantly.

Late Detection

Configurable cutoff time auto-flags latecomers. Set it once, enforce it forever.

Data Export

Download as CSV or copy to Google Sheets in a single click.

HOW IT WORKS

Three Steps to Automation

01
Register Students
Enter name and student ID, then capture their face via webcam. Each student gets a unique biometric profile.
02
Start Scanning
Set your late cutoff time. The scanner runs continuously, identifying students and marking them present or late in real time.
03
Track & Export
Browse monthly attendance, mark exceptions, and export to CSV or Google Sheets with a single click.
